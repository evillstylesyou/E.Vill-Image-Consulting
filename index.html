Services

We offer a variety of affordable services fit for every lifestyle

Initial Consultation

Our free 15–20-minute initial consultation will allow us to get a better understanding of how we can guide you to discover your inner confidence. This service can be provided virtually or in-person.

Closet Evaluation

This service focuses on current pieces in your wardrobe as well as how to incorporate staple pieces based on each individual's lifestyle.

Styling for Events

Have a special occasion coming up? Don't know what to wear? This service will provide the right amount of inspiration to make you shine at your next big event!

Personal Shopping

Does shopping give you anxiety or unsure on what style fits you the best? We provide a one-on-one service to make shopping less of a hassle and more enjoyable. Stuck in a last-minute meeting or at dance recital? We can help you shop or pick up your outfit so you can be present in the moment.

The Creative Director

After being a stay-at-home mom for 3 years, Eliana finally reentered the workforce, however she found it overwhelming when it came down to dressing for the part. This personal experience led to the idea of wanting to create a safe space for women of all demographics, who are struggling to find their confidence due to demanding lifestyles, as well as recent life transitions. Our goal at E.Vill Image Consulting is to ensure that your journey to self-rediscovery focuses on your specific needs, is financially conscious, and fits your particular lifestyle. Eliana graduated from Mesa Community College in 2012 with a Degree in Fashion Design and Fine Arts. Her degrees were influential in her following careers as an alterations specialist, making her own designs, and styling in a designer retail environment. **How personal do I want to get?** Specialties,how I want to help community .
